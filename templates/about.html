What we used for the Tokopedia Sentiment Analysis Web App?

NLP Model

BERT transformer model developed by HuggingFace

We employed the IndoBERT variant, which was trained on data from multiple sources :

  1. Indonesian Wikipedia (74M words)
  2. News articles from Kompas, Tempo (Tala et al., 2003), and Liputan6 (55M words total)
  3. Indonesian Web Corpus (Medved and Suchomel, 2017) (90M words).

Did additional training and fine-tuning through our own labelled dataset containing 5764 data.

We use the IndoBERT model for two tasks :

  1. Aspect extraction (Accuracy : 82%)
  2. Sentiment Classifier (Accuracy : 86%)
